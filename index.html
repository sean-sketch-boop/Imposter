IMPOSTOR
The Word Game
Everyone gets a secret word. One person does not.
Find the impostor before they fool everyone.
How to play
1
Add players and pick a category. The app secretly assigns one or more impostors.
2
Each player taps their name privately to see their role. Mooshies get the secret word. Impostors do not.
3
Go around the circle. Everyone says a word related to the secret word. The impostor tries to bluff.
4
Vote on who you think the impostor is. If you catch them, mooshies win. If not, the impostor wins.
